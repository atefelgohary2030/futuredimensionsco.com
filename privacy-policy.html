Privacy Policy

Future Dimensions respects the privacy of website visitors and prospective clients. Information submitted through the contact form, email, telephone, WhatsApp, or LinkedIn is used to respond to enquiries, understand business needs, prepare consultation discussions, and maintain professional communication.

  • We collect only the information voluntarily provided by visitors.
  • We do not sell visitor or client information.
  • Information may be retained for professional follow-up and service administration.
  • Visitors may request correction or deletion of their contact information by emailing info@futuredimensionsco.com.
